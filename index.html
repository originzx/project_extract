Our Story

Founded in 2010, EXTRACT ENTERPRISE began with a simple mission: to bring the freshest, most nutritious juices to people around the world. What started as a single juice bar in California has grown into an international chain with locations across 3 continents.

We work directly with farmers to source seasonal produce at the peak of freshness. Our cold-press extraction method preserves maximum nutrients and flavor.
